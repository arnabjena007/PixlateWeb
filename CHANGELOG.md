# Changelog

## v1.0.0 - Launch
**Date**: May 20, 2026
**Tags**: New

### Features
- Real-time image pixel sorting engine with high-performance execution.
- Advanced customization: Tweak sorting thresholds, adjust dimensions, and toggle reverse operations on the fly.
- Artistic color-sorting mode to generate stunning, organic color flow patterns.
- Interactive text and image overlays with complete freedom for dragging, resizing, and layer-stacking.
- Creative filters & effects: Add vignette, scan lines, halftone textures, and customizable color overlays.
- Seamless PNG and JPG export, entirely rendered on your local device for maximum privacy.
- Effortless importing: Drag & drop, paste directly from your clipboard, or use the standard file picker.
